This month's instrument: The Kazoo

The instrument to where you make the noise

The Kazoo is rather an unique instrument in that a person hums into it in order to get it to make noise

The Kazoo is known as a membranophone instrument, which is a family of instruments to where a vibrating membrane makes a noise

Come in and sign up for a free Kazoo demonstration Saturday, September 5 at 11:00 a.m.

The Kazoo originates in Africa and dates back hundreds of years

Kazoo's can be made out of metal, plastic.
