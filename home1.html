Welcome to <<THE MUSIC STORE>>

Here at THE MUSIC STORE, we have the finest selection of instruments and accessories. Whether you want to be a proud new owner of a guitar, piano, drums, a ukulele(a small four-stringed guitar of Hawaiian origin), a trombone, a mandolin or even a kazoo, you have come to the right place.

Music stores arose to service the needs of the local community. In addition to having a wide variety of instruments, we offer a whole host of music courses, to help you learn to play your new instrument well.

And we have great collection of music books, sheet music, and other music accessories.

We currently offer courses for the following instruments:
  • Guitar
  • Piano
  • Drums
Plus, one Saturday a month we have a free introductory course on an instrument
